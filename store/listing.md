## Description (detailed, under 1400 characters recommended)

Big words getting in the way? Twelvify fixes that.

Just highlight any confusing text on any webpage, click the wand icon, and get back a clear, plain-English version — powered by AI. No copy-pasting. No switching tabs. It just works, right there on the page.

**What Twelvify does:**
• Simplifies complex sentences, jargon, and academic language
• Works on any website — news articles, research papers, Wikipedia, emails, legal docs
• Delivers results instantly using streaming AI (no waiting for a full response)
• Lets you tune the simplification to your reading level in Settings

**Why people use it:**
• Reading a research paper and hitting a wall of terminology
• Getting through dense news articles faster
• Understanding contracts and legal language without a law degree
• Learning new topics without needing a dictionary every other sentence

Twelvify is powered by AI and built for anyone who wants to read the internet without stopping every two sentences to Google a word.

Free to use. No account required.
