Scaling Transformer Architectures for Multilingual Language Understanding Tasks
K. Yamamoto, R. Patel, L. Okonkwo, M. Schneider
Submitted 22 February 2026
Abstract
Recent advancements in transformer-based architectures have demonstrated remarkable efficacy in natural language processing tasks, achieving state-of-the-art performance across multiple benchmark evaluations. Our approach leverages a hierarchical attention mechanism combined with adaptive tokenization to address cross-lingual transfer limitations observed in prior work. Experimental results on GLUE, SuperGLUE, and XQuAD datasets indicate significant improvements over baseline models.
